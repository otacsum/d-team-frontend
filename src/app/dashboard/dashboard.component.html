About the LAUSD

Accredited by the Higher Learning Commission (www.hlcommission.org), LAUSD offers a variety of learning options to new students, transfer students, working professionals, and active and retired military. At LAUSD's California campuses in Los Angeles, Burbank, and Sacremento, or via the Internet in homes and offices around the world, A LAUSD education is designed around a busy life.

LAUSD History

The LAUSD was founded by four members of CS492 at Colorado Technical University, Anthony Hoffmeyer, Bryce Howell, Michael Muscato, and Hollie Nelson, with the intent of demonstrating a full-featured school management system. The application is created on Node.js using the Nest.js back-end framework, Angular.js front-end framework, and a Postgres SQL Database.
